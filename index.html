Our Features

Key features of our erp

  • Mobile Responsive

    Generally, the AlterSys ERP system is web-based and mobile friendly. The management used to our existing system can provide all authorization anywhere using their smartphone. By having an ERP system adaptable to mobile devices, businesses can keep their data organized and easily accessible.

  • Simple integration

    AlterSys integrates ERP systems to easily track and monitor various processes, including customer orders, inventory and customer data, and credit card processing. This strong integration system, Provides accurate data to all departments, Significantly reduces cost as unnecessary processes are eliminated, and Customer-specific data is available in real-time.

  • Automation of business operations

    AlterSys ERP solutions fully automate repetitive tasks such as order entry, accounting, billing, and analysis, among others. Automating these systems allows employees to devote less time to these processes, enabling them to devote their attention to more critical tasks.

  • Real-time Reporting and analytics

    AlterSys ERP software includes real-time reporting as a standard feature. Using this software Collects the data at every point of contact, down to the individual customer interaction.

  • Better inter-departmental collaboration

    AlterSys ERP software Inter-departmental collaboration is ensuring that all departments within an organization have the same view of the data and processes within the system. By working together, departments can provide assurances that all data entry and processing is accurate and consistent.

  • Increased security and quality of data

    Altersys ERP software is widely used by businesses of all sizes to manage their operations. The software provides increased security and quality of data by encrypting all data at rest and in transit. In addition, the software uses a Byzantine Fault Tolerance algorithm to ensure that even if one node in the system fails, the system can continue functioning.

  • Help maintain compliance

    The best way to ensure compliance with AlterSys ERP software is to have an in-house expert fully versed in the software's requirements and procedures. They can help ensure that all changes are made correctly and that any pending changes are completed as quickly as possible.

  • Strengthens Customer Relationships

    Strengthens customer relationships in alterSys software by automating customer interactions and tracking progress. Altersys customers can view detailed reports on their interactions, including how many tickets they opened, closed, replied to, or escalated. These reports help managers the success of their customer relations strategies and identify areas for improvement.

Frequently asked questions

What is Altersys ERP software?

Altersys ERP software is a business management software that helps companies to manage their business

What is the difference between Altersys ERP software and other software?

ERP stands for Enterprise Resource Planning. Enterprise resource planning technically is a type of software, the only difference between ERP and other simple software is that ERP is not for one module or section of organization but for whole, it integrates the entire functioning of the organization to make it work as a unit.

What is the difference between ERP software and CRM software?

While the entire organization will come to rely on both ERP and CRM systems, the fundamental difference between ERP and CRM is that ERP is primarily for financial data and the finance department, while CRM is customer data used by the sales and customer service departments.
